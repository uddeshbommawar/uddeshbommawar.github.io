## What is Search Engine Optimization (SEO)?

### Why SEO is important

SEO helps businesses attract organic traffic, improve brand credibility, and generate long-term growth.

## How Search Engines Work

Search engines crawl, index, and rank web pages based on relevance and quality.

### Crawling and indexing

Search engines use bots to discover and store content across the web.

## Types of SEO

There are three main types of SEO you should understand.

### On-page SEO

Optimizing content, headings, internal links, and metadata.

### Technical SEO

Improving site speed, crawlability, and overall technical health.

### Off-page SEO

Building authority through backlinks and brand mentions.
